Smart Image Analysis

Runs entirely in your browser — no uploads, no API keys. Analyzes pixel data (histogram, colour buckets, edge energy, Laplacian focus & gray-world balance) to suggest enhancements you can apply with one click.

① Auto-Enhance

Auto-levels from the luma histogram — corrects flat contrast, dull exposure & weak colour.

② Smart Compress

Recommends an optimal format & quality from image detail (edge density) and transparency.

③ Colour Palette

Dominant colours extracted from the image. Click a swatch to use it as the flatten/matte background.

④ Smart Auto-Crop

Finds the most visually interesting region (edge-energy saliency) honouring your current crop aspect ratio.

⑤ Sharpness Check

Scores focus via the variance-of-Laplacian. Soft / blurry images get a gentle contrast bump to crisp them up.

⑥ Auto White-Balance

Detects a colour cast (gray-world) and suggests an approximate corrective tint to neutralise it.

⑦ Smart Resize

Recommends a web-friendly output size for oversized images (long edge ≈ 1920px), preserving aspect ratio.
